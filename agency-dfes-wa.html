01Executive Summary

DFES manages emergency services and aviation support across Western Australia. Official grant information confirms a $1.221 million project to establish a remotely piloted natural-hazard monitoring capability and identify and procure a suitable fixed-wing RPA for defined use cases. The selected platform, operational date, ReOC arrangements and BVLOS approvals are not publicly confirmed.

02Why this matters

DFES is one of the strongest confirmed Australian examples of a public-safety agency investing in an RPAS capability designed around wide-area natural-hazard intelligence.

04Operational Context

DFES already coordinates substantial crewed aviation capability. The funded remotely piloted capability is intended to provide lower-cost aerial intelligence, situational awareness and safety benefits across emergency management.
