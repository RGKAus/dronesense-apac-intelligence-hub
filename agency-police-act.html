01Executive Summary

ACT Policing is the community policing arm of the Australian Federal Police (AFP), delivering local policing services to the Australian Capital Territory under an agreement between the ACT Government and the Commonwealth. No dedicated RPAS/drone program specific to ACT Policing (as distinct from the AFP nationally, or from other states' Air Wings) was identified in this research pass.

02Why this matters

As a comparatively small jurisdiction policed via an AFP arrangement rather than a standalone state police force, ACT Policing's RPAS posture (if any) may be delivered through AFP national capability or through mutual-aid arrangements with NSW Police rather than a dedicated ACT fleet — this is an open research question rather than an assumption either way.
