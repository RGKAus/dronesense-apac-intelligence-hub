01Executive Summary

AMSA coordinates Australia’s national aviation and maritime search and rescue service across a very large search and rescue region. Its published capability is centred on coordination, contracted aircraft and assets of opportunity. A current in-house RPAS program, ReOC, BVLOS capability or drone fleet is not publicly confirmed in the reviewed official sources.

02Why this matters

AMSA is a critical mission coordinator for any future integration of RPAS into national maritime or aviation search and rescue, even where aircraft are supplied by partner agencies or contractors.
