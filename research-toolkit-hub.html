2. Research Methodology

  1. ScopeWrite one answerable question.
  2. Primary sourceRegulator or org site first — see table above.
  3. Cross-checkTwo independent sources → confirmed.
  4. Date itRecord access date, not just publish date.
  5. Rate honestlyUse the six levels below — don't inflate.
  6. Cross-linkAdd to every relevant hub.
RatingMeaning
ConfirmedPrimary source, or 2 independent confirmations
Highly likelyOne credible source, not cross-checked
Partially verifiedSome elements confirmed, others inferred
UnverifiedNot yet researched
HistoricalWas accurate; no longer current
SupersededReplaced by newer reg/program/org

Source validation

  • Primary source, not an aggregator
  • Publish/last-updated date visible
  • Independent second source agrees
  • Still current — not superseded

4. Research Checklists

Agency research

  • RPAS team
  • Aviation unit
  • Procurement
  • Dock deployments
  • BVLOS
  • Regulations
  • Strategic priorities
  • Contacts

Company research

  • Organisation type
  • HQ & regions
  • Confirmed customers
  • Products shipping vs roadmap
  • Funding / ownership
  • Recent announcements

Regulation research

  • Clause / section cited
  • Regulator's own source
  • Current status
  • Open consultations
  • Access date recorded
  • Cross-linked to agencies

Technology research

  • Maturity level
  • Regulatory treatment
  • Providers, sourced
  • Key constraint
  • Mission cross-links

Mission profile research

  • Agencies confirmed
  • Regulatory pathway
  • Sensor / payload
  • Real case study
  • Outcome / response-time data
  • Cross-links

Quick-scan triage

  • Stakeholders
  • Drone programmes
  • Procurement opportunities
  • Legislation
  • Operational capability
  • Technology partners
