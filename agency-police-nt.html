01Executive Summary

No dedicated Northern Territory Police RPAS/drone program was identified in this research pass. The confirmed drone activity located in the Northern Territory during this research (Operation LUNAR, maritime surveillance) belongs to the federal Australian Border Force, not NT Police — these should not be conflated.

02Why this matters

NT's remote and vast geography (similar rationale to the ABF's own stated case for RPAS in the Territory) suggests NT Police could be a strong candidate for RPAS/DFR-style capability, but this is a hypothesis, not a confirmed fact — worth flagging for direct follow-up rather than assuming based on the ABF's unrelated northern Australia deployment.
