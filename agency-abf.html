01Executive Summary

The Australian Border Force protects Australia’s border and maritime domain. In January 2026 ABF publicly confirmed a broader RPAS rollout across northern Australia, including operations supporting detection of illegal foreign fishing. Public material confirms day-and-night sensing and real-time video, but does not identify the aircraft, software platform, ReOC status or BVLOS approval pathway.

02Why this matters

ABF operates across vast, remote and maritime environments where persistent aerial intelligence can extend patrol reach and reduce the time needed to investigate suspected activity.
